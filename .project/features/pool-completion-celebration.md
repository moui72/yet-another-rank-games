When a comparison pool becomes fully deterministically ordered (no ties/uncertainty remain), show a confetti celebration animation and hide the comparison controls until a game is removed from the ranking.
Why: once the pool is fully resolved there's nothing left to compare, so continuing to show comparison controls is misleading; celebrating completion gives positive feedback for finishing a ranking.
